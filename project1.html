Style Ideas

Chic Style

Elegance and class minus vibrant colours. They are monochromatic pieces with sleek lines which excludes power. These are clothings in black, white and neutrals saving colour bold accessories.

Casual Style

These are informal wears. Number one point when it comes to casual wears, is to focus on thr fit and comfort. Feel cozy, dress to be relaxed and happy. Mix and match with textures for a laidback.

Business Casual Style

A less formal than traditional business clothing but still professional enough to be office appropriate. The key is to always look polished and professional. Avoid revealing clothes and work together with versatility.

African Prints

They are industrially produced colorful cotton cloths with batik-inspired printing. One feature of these materials is the lack of difference in the color intensity of the front and back sides. They can be sorted into categories of quality due to the processes of manufacturing.

Business Professional Wears

This attire is used in a more conservative settings or companies with strict dress codes. These includes tidy dresses, skirts or slacks. Top should include neat button-down shirts or blouses with blazers. Shoes includes classic heels, loafers, flats, minimal jewelries and belts.
